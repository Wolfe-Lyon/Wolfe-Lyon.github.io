23461: Draw-2-×-1-blocks flag
Flag that determines whether or not to only draw the top two characters in 2 × 2 blocks. The instructions between 41347 and 41351 inclusive are set up to skip rendering of the lower two character cells in a 2 × 2 block if this value is not zero, however this flag is never modified, meaning that 2 × 1 blocks are never drawn.
