Bit Effect
0 Unused
1 Unused

4 Block cannot be passed vertically (upwards) by Magic Knight. Magic Knight will "bang his head" while jumping if block is above him.
5 Block must have its attribute preserved (i.e. Magic Knight's attribute is overridden when passing through)
6 Block cannot be passed horizontally by Magic Knight (impassable from sides, i.e. a wall)
7 Block cannot be passed vertically (downwards) by Magic Knight. Magic Knight can stand on the block if it is below him.
