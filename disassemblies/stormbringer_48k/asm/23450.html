23450: Vertical and horizontal mirror flags
Used by the routines at 61214, 61236, 61267, 61297, 61336, 61418 and 61521 in graphic / room drawing. When the apply-vertical-mirror flag is set, the graphic is reflected in a vertical mirror (i.e. left and right are swapped). When the apply-horizontal-mirror flag is set, the graphic is reflected in a horizontal mirror (i.e. top and bottom are swapped).
