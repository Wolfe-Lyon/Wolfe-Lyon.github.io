24898: Characters' current positions
Eight groups of three bytes, one group per character (excluding Magic Knight). The values are:
Offset Effect
0 Room number
1 x-coordinate
2 y-coordinate
Bit 7 of the room number field is the arrival-pending flag. If this is set, and the remaining bits correspond to Magic Knight's current room, then that character will appear in a puff of smoke.
See also 48661 for characters' initial positions
