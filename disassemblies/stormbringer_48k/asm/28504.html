Referenced by Table of Block Style Definitions at 49794.
Four of these UDGs are also used for a different purpose, i.e. floor drawing. In the floor drawing routine at 39393, the instruction at 39428 (a call to 63488) changes character printing routine to use base address of Floor Character Set graphics (28248). As a result, entries 20, 21, 22 and 23 in this table correspond to floor UDGs 52, 53, 54 and 55 respectively.
