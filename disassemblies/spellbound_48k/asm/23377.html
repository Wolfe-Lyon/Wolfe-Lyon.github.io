23377: Gimbal-White-Out Safe Flag
Controls whether or not the "GIMBAL IS NOT YET RELEASED..." warning window appears when a command is selected.
Value Effect
0 attempting to execute the command with Gimbal as the subject will bring up the warning window
Any other value command will always be successful (or at least its success will be independent of whether or not Gimbal is free)
