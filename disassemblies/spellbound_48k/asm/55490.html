55490: Characters' rates of stat regeneration
Stats are in order:
Offset Meaning
0, 4 Strength
1, 5 Happiness
2, 6 Stamina
3, 7 Spell power
  • First four bytes are negative values and are the amount each stat is reduced by each cycle while character is awake (tiring)
  • Last four bytes are positive values and are the amount each stat is increased by each cycle while character is asleep (regenerating)
