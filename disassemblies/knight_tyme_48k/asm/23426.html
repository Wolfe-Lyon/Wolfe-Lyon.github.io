23426: Miscellaneous data, part 3
These values are temporary stores for x- and y-coordinates used in window drawing and graphic rendering as part of the room drawing process.
23426 DEFB 0 x-coordinate of left edge (of frame, or of interior) of window (characters) / graphic (room drawing)
23427 DEFB 0 y-coordinate of top edge (of frame, or of interior) of window (characters) / graphic (room drawing)
23428 DEFB 0 x-coordinate of right edge (of frame, or of interior) of window (characters), x+1 for current graphic (room drawing)
23429 DEFB 0 y-coordinate of bottom edge (of frame, or of interior) of window (characters), y+1 for current graphic (room drawing)
