41550: Script Routine (63): Have Skeleton Advance or Retreat Based Upon Positions of Berk and Fake Boni
The skeleton will step forward once, and then step back, if Berk is close enough and Fake Boni is in a room to the right. If Fake Boni is in the Skeleton's room, or a room to the left of this, then the Skeleton will step forward repeatedly while Berk is close enough and will only back off when Berk moves out of range. The range is nine, so if the distance between Berk's right edge and the Skeleton's left edge is less than nine then the Skeleton will advance. A distance of nine or more will see the Skeleton retreat. Note that the calculated range is based upon the Skeleton's state data at 45404, in which the x-coordinates of his left and right edges do not match his apparent position or width graphically.
